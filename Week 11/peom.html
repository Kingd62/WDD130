Beneath the sky, so vast and wide, A journey waits, where dreams abide.

Through fields of gold and rivers clear, A path unknown, yet drawing near

A quiet thought, a passing glance, The world spins on in endless dance.

And though we wander far apart, The road we walk is in our heart

Through moonlit nights, the stars do gleam, A steady guide for those who dream.

The heavens open, paths unfold, A story written, brave and bold.

With every step, a tale is told, Of fleeting dreams and hands to hold.

The world turns softly, yet so fast, Each moment lost, but never past.

So take my hand, and don’t look back, We’ll find the way through every crack.

For in our hearts, we are alive, Together strong, together we’ll thrive.

The moonlit path begins to glow, And still, we wonder where we’ll go

So fear not, friend, the road ahead, For you’re not lost,nor left for dead.

The journey’s long, but worth the ride, We walk together, side by side.
